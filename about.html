Me

I’m a young and driven web designer and developer, raised in the most innovative place in the world (the Silicon Valley!), and now living in the most beautiful place in the world (San Diego!). I love family, friends, food, and designing and building beautiful things for the web.

My Services

Responsive Design and Development

I practice responsive web design. We can talk about the technicalities later, but basically, I’ll make your website easy to use and beautiful to look at... no matter what device it is being viewed on.

WordPress

It’s not just what your cousin uses to write his blog about cats... it’s the most widely-used and supported "content management system" in the world. This means that your website can be easily maintained and updated... by you! In other words, you won’t have to keep paying me to come back and update your website!

My Workflow

My projects iterate through four stages:

Discovery

Questions. This where I ask questions... and we figure out the answers. Who are your primary users? What are their needs? What are your needs? What content and features does your website need to ensure those needs are met? This is where we will develop the “structure” of your website.

Design

This is where I get creative! We will address the user experience, design interfaces/wireframes, and design the visual aspects of the website.

Development

This is the nerdy part. Here, I will take our designs and convert them into code, making them deployable on the web, and ensure that they are cross-browser compatible.

Distribution

The end is near! Here we will review the work, answer any questions that you have, and deploy your website! I will also provide any training necessary for you to take care of your new web presence.

Feel free to hire for me for all of these phases... or just one! (...or, you know... any amount in between!)
